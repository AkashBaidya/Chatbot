# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## About Us
Acme Corp was founded in 2010 and is a leading provider of software solutions.
We have offices in Berlin, New York, and Singapore.

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
Flexible working hours are available with manager approval.
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to treat colleagues with respect and professionalism.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.
Confidential information must never be shared outside the company.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year — in June and December.
Employees are evaluated on: technical skills, collaboration, and initiative.

## Equipment Policy
Employees receive a laptop and peripherals upon joining.
Equipment must be returned upon leaving the company.
Lost or damaged equipment should be reported to IT within 24 hours.

## Communication Tools
- **Slack**: For day-to-day communication
- **Jira**: For project and task tracking
- **Confluence**: For documentation and wikis
- **Zoom**: For video meetings
