ACME CORP EMPLOYEE BENEFITS GUIDE
===================================

HEALTH INSURANCE
- Medical: Fully covered for employee; 80% covered for dependents
- Dental: Covered up to €1,500/year
- Vision: Covered up to €300/year
- Mental Health: 12 therapy sessions per year fully covered

VACATION & TIME OFF
- Standard vacation: 25 days per year (prorated for new hires)
- Sick leave: Unlimited, with a doctor's note after 3 consecutive days
- Public holidays: All local public holidays are observed
- Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregiver; 4 weeks for secondary
- Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family

RETIREMENT & FINANCIAL
- Company pension: Employer contributes 5% of gross salary
- Stock options: Available after 1 year of employment
- Annual bonus: Performance-based, typically 5-15% of base salary
- Referral bonus: €2,000 for a successful hire referral

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
- Learning budget: €1,500 per employee per year
- Conference attendance: Up to 2 conferences per year
- Internal training: Weekly tech talks and monthly workshops

WELLNESS
- Gym membership: Subsidized up to €50/month
- Ergonomic setup: One-time €500 allowance for home office setup
- Bike-to-work scheme: Leased e-bike option available

FOOD & OFFICE PERKS
- Free lunch in the office canteen Monday through Thursday
- Fully stocked snack kitchen
- Monthly team dinners (budget: €50/person)
